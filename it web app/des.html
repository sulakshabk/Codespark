Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke, also known as Shinchan, is the main protagonist of the series. He is a mischievous and energetic 5-year-old boy with a wild imagination. He loves to play pranks, is fond of candy and snacks, and has a peculiar way of talking that often gets him into funny situations.

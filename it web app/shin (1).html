Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke, also known as Shinchan, is the main protagonist of the series. He is a mischievous and energetic 5-year-old boy with a wild imagination. He loves to play pranks, is fond of candy and snacks, and has a peculiar way of talking that often gets him into funny situations.

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi is Shinchan's caring and hardworking father. He works at the Kasukabe Defense Force and often faces humorous challenges at home, thanks to Shinchan's mischievous nature, adding a comedic touch to the family dynamics.

Mitsi

Mitsi

Mitsi is Shinchan's caring and hardworking mother. She plays a vital role in maintaining the Nohara family's harmony and often deals with the humorous situations caused by Shinchan's antics.

Himawari Nohara

Himawari Nohara

Himawari is Shinchan's adorable younger sister. Despite being a baby, she sometimes displays intelligence beyond her years. She is often amused by her brother's mischievous antics, bringing a cute and entertaining element to the Nohara family dynamics.

Shiro

Shiro

Shiro is a white pup Shinchan found in a cardboard box. While he is a beloved member of the family, he is the subject of neglect thanks to his owner's unintentional oversight, adding a touch of comedic irony to the Nohara household.

Kazama

Kazama

Kazama is one of Shinchan's classmates and friends. He is a smart and studious boy who often finds himself at odds with Shinchan's mischievous behavior, creating a humorous contrast in their friendship.

Nene Sakurada

Nene Sakurada

Nene is another one of Shinchan's friends and classmates. She is a kind-hearted and sensitive girl who enjoys spending time with her friends. She often finds herself amused by Shinchan's mischievous antics, contributing to the lively dynamic of their friendship.

Masao Sato

Masao Sato

Masao is one of Shinchan's close friends and classmates. He is a timid and gentle boy who is easily scared by Shinchan's pranks but remains a loyal friend, adding a touch of innocence to the group's dynamic.

Bo Suzuki

Bo Suzuki

Bo is a friendly and easygoing boy who is often seen with Masao. He enjoys participating in various activities with his friends and often joins Shinchan in his playful escapades, contributing to the lively atmosphere of their friendship.

Ai Suotome

Ai Suotome

Ai is a new student who joins Shinchan's class later in the series. She is born in New York and is from an extremely rich family. She is always seen radiating sophistication and adds a touch of glamour to the class dynamic.

Midori Ishizaka

Midori Ishizaka

She is a 24 years old teacher in Sunflower class. Yoshinaga is her maiden name. She is always in bitter rivalry with Ume Matsuzaka and they get into banter that keeps the school staff entertained.

Ume Matsuzaka

Ume Matsuzaka

Ume is a teacher in Shinchan's school and she is single. She likes to live stylishly in public but she actually struggles to save her money for accessories, providing a humorous contrast to her outward appearance.

Characters

Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke Nohara

Shinnosuke, also known as Shinchan, is the main protagonist of the series. He is a mischievous and energetic 5-year-old boy with a wild imagination. He loves to play pranks, is fond of candy and snacks, and has a peculiar way of talking that often gets him into funny situations.

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi Nohara

Hiroshi is Shinchan's caring and hardworking father. He works at the Kasukabe Defense Force and often faces humorous challenges at home, thanks to Shinchan's mischievous nature, adding a comedic touch to the family dynamics.

Mitsi Nohara

Mitsi Nohara

Mitsi is Shinchan's patient and loving mother. She manages the household chores while dealing with Shinchan's playful mischief. Misae is a caring and dedicated mother, often finding humor in the chaos that comes with raising a spirited son.
